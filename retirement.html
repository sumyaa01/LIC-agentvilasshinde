Retirement & Pension Policies

LIC Jeevan Akshay VII

An immediate annuity plan offering pension from the next month of investment.

  • Minimum Purchase Price: ₹1 Lakh (Offline), ₹1.5 Lakhs (Online)
  • Options for lifetime or joint-life annuity
  • Guaranteed lifelong pension
  • No medical required

LIC New Jeevan Shanti

A deferred annuity plan that provides guaranteed pension starting after a few years.

  • Single Premium payment
  • Choose deferment period: 1 to 12 years
  • Options for single or joint-life pension
  • Pension starts after deferment period

Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

Government pension scheme for unorganized sector workers.

  • Age: 18–40 years
  • Pension: ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 per month after 60 years
  • Government co-contribution for eligible subscribers
  • Guaranteed monthly pension on retirement

National Pension System (NPS)

Market-linked retirement savings plan for all Indian citizens.

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Partial withdrawal allowed
  • Tax benefits under 80C and 80CCD(1B)
  • 40% corpus must be used to buy annuity at retirement
