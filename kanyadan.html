Kanyadan Policy Details

LIC Kanyadan Policy is a special plan designed to financially support your daughter's marriage and education. It is a customized version of the LIC Jeevan Lakshya plan.

Key Features:

  • Designed for fathers of girl children
  • Premium payment term is less than policy term
  • High returns with risk coverage
  • Tax benefits under 80C & 10(10D)

Example Plan:

  • Age of father: 30 years
  • Monthly Premium: ₹1,500 approx.
  • Policy Term: 25 years
  • Premium Paying Term: 20 years
  • Maturity Amount: ₹10 – ₹15 Lakhs (approx. based on bonus)

Benefits:

  • Lump sum at maturity
  • Premium waiver on death of father
  • Annual income benefit till maturity
